SilentSignal: Noiseless Vehicle Horn System

This project is designed to reduce noise pollution caused by honking on busy roads. The system aims to replace traditional vehicle horns with an internal communication network between vehicles, where drivers and passengers can receive alerts through an internal notification system. This way, honking is only heard by the people inside the vehicles, helping reduce distractions and contributing to a quieter environment.
